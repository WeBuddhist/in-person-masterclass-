# WeBuddhist In-Person Masterclass — Pune 2026

# Overview

The WeBuddhist In-Person Masterclass is a 5-day immersive training programme for Buddhist content creators, held in Pune, India. Delivered in partnership with Triratna Buddhist Community, this masterclass equips participants with the skills, curriculum, and community to share the Dharma skillfully across digital platforms.

📅 Dates: July 15–19, 2026

📍 Primary Venue: Yashada Institute, Pune (200 participants — subject to Bharti funding)

📍 Stretch Venue: Triratna Retreat Center, Lonavala (100 participants max)

🌐 Language: English with Hindi translation (translators provided by Triratna)

🤝 Collaborating Organisation: Triratna Buddhist Community 🧭 Primary Triratna Contact: Dr. Gaikwad
